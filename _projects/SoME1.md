Youtube math-comunicator Grant Sanderson, alias [3Blue1Brown](https://www.3blue1brown.com/), held the first "Summer of Maths Exposition", SoME 1, back in 2021.

I participated with the following project, which was in the form of this [pdf](https://happyves.github.io/assets/pdf/3b1bexpov2.pdf), which gives some cool ways to build surfaces that span a given knot.

Unfortunately, I didn't win, but the winners were indeed hard to compete with, as you can check [here](https://www.3blue1brown.com/blog/some1-results).

The offer of animating the project with [manim](https://www.manim.community/) is still open, though it's quite a challenge.
