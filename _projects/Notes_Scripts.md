Here are some little notes I wrote. They are based on books, lectures and papers that I tried to reformulate in my own words, often attempting to both fill in missing technical details and to provide a conceptual understanding of the content.

__Warning:__ These notes are probably full of spelling/grammar mistakes and typos, and sometimes incomplete. Before reading a section, you may want to look for words such as "COMPLETE" or "FIX" at the beginning of paragraphs, near the end of sections: if such a word appears, it's either that I didn't finish the exposition due to lack of time and interest, or that I noted a mistake that I know how or have time to fix.

In any case, I hope you enjoy the clarification work I tried to make:

* [Algorithms](https://happyves.github.io/assets/pdf/OptiAlgoBook.pdf) : It's a huge set of notes on all kinds of algorithmic flavoured topics, including combinatorial, linear-integer and convex optimization, numerical, symbolic, computational math, some AI and some game theory. It contains some solved exercises. __Last update:__ 30.07.2023

* [Conversational combinatorics](https://happyves.github.io/assets/pdf/ConversationalCombinatorics.pdf) : These are notes on (mostly extremal) combinatorics.  __Last update:__ 30.07.2023
