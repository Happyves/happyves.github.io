Matching is the task of making pairs of objects under certain constraints.

For example, when I first applied for university in France, the admission procedure was on a website on which students all over the nation selected a list universities at which they'd like to study.
They would then provide their grades and other information, based on which universities made a list of which students they'd like to have on their course. Finally, an algorithm decided how to match students with universities.
In this scenario, constraints on the matching could be, for example, matching students and universities that were on each others respective lists, making sure a maximum number of pairs are obtained, or taking the students and universities preferences into account.
